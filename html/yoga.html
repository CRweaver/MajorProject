YOGA

Yoga is an amazing tool to connect with your body, slow your mind and restore balance.

+ YIN YOGA

A more passive style of yoga focusing on slow, deep stretching. Postures are held for 3-10 minutes on average. Meditation through stillness.

+ VINYASA/FLOW YOGA

A more active style of yoga linking each posture with the breath to develop strength, balance and flexibility. Meditation through movement.
